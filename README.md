# How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your account associated with our app, please follow the instructions below:

Go to Profile -> Delete -> permanently delete my account and all data.

If need more help.

1. **Send an email** to `startupguystudio.team@gmail.com` with the subject: "Delete My Account".
2. Include your **registered email** and **username** (if applicable).
3. Your account will be permanently deleted within 7 working days.

For questions or privacy concerns, contact us at the same email address.
